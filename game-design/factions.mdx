# Factions

Each game uses exactly as many factions as there are players (3–5), drawn randomly from the roster of 5. Each player is
assigned exactly one faction secretly at game start. No two players share a faction.

## Faction goals and win conditions

| Faction             | Goal                                              | Win condition                                               |
|---------------------|---------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------|
| ⚡ **Erasers**       | Wipe outcomes completely                          | Annihilate 4+ outcomes OR reach 20 pts                      |
| 📜 **Prophets**     | Events resolve as written                         | 5+ events resolve as written OR reach 20 pts                |
| 🔄 **Revisionists** | Secret preferred outcome changes each era         | Secret outcome wins 3+ eras OR reach 20 pts                 |
| 🧵 **Weavers**      | Build causal chains across eras                   | Complete a chain of 3+ connected events OR reach 20 pts     |
| 📣 **Activists**    | Publicly declare outcome, score double if correct | Correct declarations in 3+ consecutive eras OR reach 20 pts |

## Special actions

Each faction has 3 unique special actions always available regardless of hand. Each action round: play **1 card OR 1
special — never both**.

### ⚡ Erasers

| Special        | Effect                                                              |
|----------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Annihilate** | Remove one outcome from an event entirely this era                  |
| **Corrupt**    | Invert the probability effect of another player's card this round   |
| **Cascade**    | Carry an erasure forward to the same outcome next era automatically |

### 📜 Prophets

| Special         | Effect                                                                       |
|-----------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Foresight**   | See next era's future events before anyone else                              |
| **Seal**        | Lock one outcome's probability — no cards can modify it until end of round   |
| **Fulfillment** | Publicly declare: if written outcome wins, your score doubles for that event |

### 🔄 Revisionists

| Special     | Effect                                                                      |
|-------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Rewrite** | Swap secret preferred outcome to any available outcome                      |
| **Mimic**   | Copy the probability effect of any card played by another player this round |
| **Obscure** | Hide faction identity for one round — appears as a random other faction     |

### 🧵 Weavers

| Special      | Effect                                                                      |
|--------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Thread**   | Declare a causal link between current and a past outcome to grow the chain  |
| **Tapestry** | Protect next chain link from Annihilate once per era (requires chain of 2+) |
| **Unravel**  | Break another player's chain, resetting their chain progress                |

### 📣 Activists

| Special      | Effect                                                                                   |
|--------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Rally**    | Publicly declare an outcome at round start; all probability shifts boosted 50% toward it |
| **Expose**   | Reveal another player's actions from last round (event and shift direction)              |
| **Momentum** | Carry a +10% probability bonus if declared outcome won last era                          |
